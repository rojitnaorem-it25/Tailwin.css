Camera & Imaging

  • Many Parrot Anafi models focus on aerial photography and videography with high-resolution cameras.

  • The standard Anafi features a Sony-based camera with HDR support, capable of shooting 4K UHD video and up to 21 MP photos. It includes a tilting gimbal (up to 180°) for unique upward or downward angles and lossless digital zoom in select modes.

  • Advanced versions like the Parrot ANAFI Ai add a 48 MP sensor, 6× zoom with lossless modes, and enhanced stabilization.

    • Advanced Variants & Features

  • Some models in the Anafi lineup are geared for professional or enterprise use:

  • Parrot Anafi 4K Thermal Drone and Parrot Anafi Thermal Drone include thermal imaging alongside standard cameras for inspections and surveying.

  • Parrot Anafi Ai USA Thermal Drone combines thermal and advanced capabilities with secure features and extended professional use cases.

    • Control & Software

  • Parrot drones typically pair with the FreeFlight mobile app for flight planning, camera control, and smart modes.

  • Some advanced models (like Anafi Ai) support 4G connectivity, obstacle avoidance sensors, and SDKs for custom applications.

    • Flight & Performance

  • Flight times vary by model — the original Anafi has around 25 minutes per charge, while newer variants like the AI model offer up to about 32 minutes.

  • Typical max speeds are up to ~55 km/h (about 15 m/s), with decent wind resistance for stable flight.

  • Many units support GPS/GLONASS positioning and smart flight features (like return-to-home and follow modes) via Parrot’s FreeFlight app.
